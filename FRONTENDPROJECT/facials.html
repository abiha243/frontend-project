Aftercare Instructions

To maximize your facial results and maintain healthy skin, follow these guidelines:

Wear Sunscreen

Apply SPF 30+ daily as your skin may be more sensitive after treatment.

Gentle Cleansing

Use only recommended cleansers for 24-48 hours post-facial.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to help flush toxins and keep skin hydrated.

Avoid Heavy Makeup

Allow skin to breathe for at least 12 hours after your facial.

Skip Active Ingredients

Avoid retinoids and acids for 24 hours unless directed otherwise.

Postpone Intense Workouts

Avoid sweating heavily for 12 hours to prevent irritation.

Facial FAQs

Answers to common questions about our facial treatments

How often should I get a facial?

For optimal results, we recommend facials every 4-6 weeks. This aligns with the skin's natural renewal cycle. Those with specific concerns like acne may benefit from more frequent treatments initially.

Is there any downtime after a facial?

Most facials have no downtime, though you may experience slight redness immediately after. More intensive treatments like peels may require 1-2 days of recovery. Your esthetician will advise you based on your specific treatment.

Can I get a facial if I have acne?

Yes! In fact, regular facials can significantly help acne-prone skin. We offer specialized acne treatments with gentle extractions and antibacterial products. Avoid facials during severe breakouts or if using Accutane.

What's the difference between basic and advanced facials?

Basic facials focus on cleansing and relaxation, while advanced facials target specific concerns with specialized techniques and high-performance products. Advanced facials are longer and incorporate more treatment steps.

Should men get facials?

Absolutely! Men's skin is thicker and produces more oil, requiring specialized care. Our Gentleman's Facial addresses shaving irritation, ingrown hairs, and other male-specific concerns.
