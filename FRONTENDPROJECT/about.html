Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a beauty destination

Where Beauty Meets Passion

Founded in 2010 by Anna Smith, ABS Salon & Spa began as a small boutique with just two styling chairs and a dream to revolutionize beauty services in our community. What started as a passion project has now blossomed into one of the most sought-after beauty destinations in the region.

Our philosophy has always been simple: to enhance natural beauty while providing an exceptional experience. Every service we offer is infused with this belief, creating transformations that go beyond skin deep.

At ABS Salon & Spa, we believe that true beauty comes from confidence. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to helping you discover your most beautiful self through personalized treatments and expert care.

"True beauty comes from confidence. Our mission is to help every client discover their most beautiful self."

- Anna Smith, Founder

Our Journey

Milestones in our beauty evolution

2010

Grand Opening

Launched our first location with 2 stylists and basic hair services in a cozy 800 sq ft space.

2014

Spa Expansion

Expanded our services to include full spa treatments, adding massage therapy and skincare specialists to our team.

2018

New Location

Moved to our current 5,000 sq ft premium location in the heart of the city, featuring 12 styling stations and 5 treatment rooms.

2023

Industry Recognition

Voted "Best Salon & Spa" by Beauty Times Magazine and featured in Top 10 Luxury Salons nationwide.
